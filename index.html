SentiMarket User Guide

🚀 Getting Started

Use the SEARCH ASSET bar at the top to switch between cryptocurrencies like Dogecoin, PEPE, SOL, and AVAX. The entire dashboard updates in real time.

📈 Reading the Chart

  • Green candles = price closed higher than it opened (bullish)
  • Red candles = price closed lower (bearish)
  • Yellow line = 20-period Moving Average (trend indicator)
  • Volume bars below show trading activity strength

Use 1H / 1D / 7D tabs to switch timeframes.

🔥 Hype Score

The Hype Score (0–100) measures social momentum by combining:

  • Mention Growth — how fast talk is spreading
  • Engagement Spike — likes, shares, comments surge
  • Sentiment Strength — positive vs. negative ratio
  • Volume Spike — on-chain trading activity

Score > 75 = Extreme Hype zone. Proceed with caution.

🤖 Query Chatbot

Ask the bot about any asset. Examples:

  • "Should I buy DOGE?"
  • "What is the PEPE sentiment?"
  • "Sell signal for SOL?"

The bot combines hype data + price signals to give a recommendation.

⭐ Your Own Assets

Track your portfolio in the Your Own Assets panel (top right). Green arrows = positive trend. The ➤ button opens trade details.

🔖 Bookmark & Alerts

Click Bookmark in the header to save the current asset. Use the Alerts button to set price & sentiment triggers that notify you when thresholds are hit.
